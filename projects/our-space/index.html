Our space

An app to share cards with your significant other. The users sign in, connect to each other accounts, then create and share cards among them.

I created this app as a gift for my significant other since We are in a long distance relationship. The idea is that each week we write each other a card about us.

I am now in the process of expanding the app so other people can create their accounts and do the same.
